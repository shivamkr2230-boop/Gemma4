Our Story

Crafted in Downtown Since 2009

It started with a single stone oven and an obsession with the perfect crust. Founded by Chef Marco Rossi, Downtown Pizza was born from years of studying authentic Neapolitan pizza-making traditions and a desire to bring that experience to the heart of Patna.

Over 14 years, we've grown from a tiny 12-seat café to a full-scale restaurant with in-house delivery — but our philosophy has never changed: use the best ingredients, never compromise on quality, and treat every guest like family.

Our dough is made fresh every morning from double-zero flour. Our tomatoes are San Marzano. Our mozzarella is hand-pulled. And our oven never sleeps.
